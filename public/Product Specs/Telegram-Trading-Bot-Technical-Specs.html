Standard Format

BUY XAUUSD @ 2145.67 SL 2140.00 TP 2155.00 SELL GOLD @ 2140.50 SL 2145.00 TP 2135.00

Range Entry Format

BUY GOLD ENTER IN = 2140 - 2145 SL = 2140 TP1 = 2150 SELL XAUUSD ENTER IN = 2145 - 2140 SL = 2150 TP1 = 2135

Casual Format

BUY GOLD NOW SL 2140 TP 2155 SELL XAUUSD QUICK SL 2145 TP 2135

Multi-TP Format
